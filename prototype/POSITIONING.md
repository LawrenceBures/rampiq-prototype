# SOI — Operational Coordination Intelligence

## One-line
Operational memory and coordination intelligence for airline ramp operations.

## What it does
SOI records every operational action as immutable memory, reconstructs
what happened during any shift, detects recurring operational patterns,
and surfaces coordination support when pressure accumulates.

## What it is NOT
- Not performance evaluation software
- Not workforce surveillance
- Not predictive AI
- Not autonomous dispatch

## What makes it different
1. **Replay fidelity** — reconstruct any operational moment from memory
2. **Explainable intelligence** — every insight traces to specific events
3. **Governance-first** — coordinators see their own context before anyone else
4. **Calm under pressure** — the system stays calm while operations become chaotic

## Strongest differentiator
The operational replay system. Rewind a shift, see how pressure evolved,
understand where coordination worked and where it didn't. No other ramp
operations tool does this.

## First pilot shape
Observation mode: radio stays primary, SOI reconstructs and learns.
No operational disruption. Value proven through replay accuracy.
